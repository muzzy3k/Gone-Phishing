Website Security Checker

Using secure websites is crucial for safeguarding your online privacy and security. Secure websites, indicated by "https://" in the URL and a padlock icon in the browser's address bar, encrypt the data exchanged between your device and the server, protecting sensitive information like login credentials and personal details from eavesdropping. To watch out for unsecure websites, be cautious when sharing personal information on sites that lack the "https://" prefix, avoid clicking on suspicious links or pop-ups, and verify the website's legitimacy by checking for contact information and reviews. Staying vigilant helps protect against potential threats and cyberattacks.
